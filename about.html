About Us

Wireless World Private Limited is India’s leading mobile retailer with its wings wide spread in South India, and their reputation extends across the nation. Wireless World sells Mobile phones & Tablets of domestic and international brands, Mobile Accessories, Data Cards, Earphones, Smart Watches, and many more Driven by the Mantra ‘Think Mobile, Think Wireless . Wireless World is located in Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry, Kerala, Karnataka.
